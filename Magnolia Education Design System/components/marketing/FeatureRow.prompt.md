An alternating marketing feature row — a hand-drawn illustration on a soft sky-gradient panel beside a tag / serif title / body / coral link. Alternate `reverse` down the page.

```jsx
<FeatureRow
  tag="Tutoring"
  title="Weekly tutoring for lifelong learning."
  body="One-on-one or small groups — we teach students how to learn."
  linkLabel="Learn more →" linkHref="tutoring.html"
  image="assets/illustrations/magnolia-tree.png" imageAlt="Students under a magnolia tree"
/>
```

- Illustrations carry baked-in whitespace; bump `imageScale` (~1.2–1.6) to fill the panel.
