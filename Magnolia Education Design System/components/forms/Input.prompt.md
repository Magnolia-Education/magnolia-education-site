A labelled text field with a soft rounded border that warms to navy on focus. Use `as="textarea"` for multi-line.

```jsx
<Input label="Your email address" type="email" placeholder="you@example.com" />
<Input label="Message" as="textarea" hint="Tell us a little about your student." />
```

- `label` and `hint` are optional; `hint` renders faint below the field.
