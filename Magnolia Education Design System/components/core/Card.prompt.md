A soft, rounded content card with a warm navy-tinted shadow — the brand's default container. Never hard-edged or boxy.

```jsx
<Card variant="plain" interactive>
  <h3>Tutoring</h3>
  <p>Personalized math tutoring for Grades 8–12.</p>
</Card>
```

- `variant`: `plain` (white), `accent` (coral-pale ground), `sky` (blue gradient), `navy` (inverted, on-dark text).
- `interactive`: adds the 2px hover lift for clickable cards.
