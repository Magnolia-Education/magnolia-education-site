A single session row for the student-hub schedule — "{subject} with {tutor}", the date/time, and a status pill (composes `Badge`).

```jsx
<SessionRow subject="Advanced Functions" tutorName="Rachit" when="Tue, Jun 10 · 4:30 PM EST" status="scheduled" />
```

- `status`: `scheduled` (coral), `completed` (green), `cancelled` (navy).
